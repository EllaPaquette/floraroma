About

Connect with us on social media, explore our blog for scent tips and inspiration, and be part of a community that celebrates the beauty of scent.

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Sophia Flores, the founder of Floraroma, embarked on this aromatic journey with a simple but profound inspiration - sophistication, beauty, sweet, Floraroma has gone through trial and error to create a business that is elegant. Going through a process to learn what scents and colors to use to create a calm experience with our products. We wanted to create an experience that reminded us of the beauty in nature, to cultivate trends and your home .

  • An Agency is Born

    From scented candles that infuse homes with delightful fragrances to body oils that add a touch of elegance to any space, we offer a wide range of products to cater to your needs. Floraroma uses high-quality materials and innovative designs to meet the growing demand for aesthetic and aromatic experiences.

  • Transition to Full Service

    With a wide array of scents to choose from, customers are finding our candles to be a source of relaxation, stress relief, and tranquility. Over five-hundred sales in the past month, people find our online presence and swift shipping options have made our products accessible to a broader audience.

  • Eco-Friendly

    We are not just passionate about scents; we are also dedicated to environmental responsibility. Our commitment to sustainability drives us to make eco-conscious choices at every stage of our business. From eco-friendly packaging to ethical sourcing practices, we aim to minimize our ecological footprint while maximizing your enjoyment of our products.
